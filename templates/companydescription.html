Role Descriptions

Sales Manager

Purpose: To drive revenue growth by leading the sales team and developing strategies to acquire new customers.

Key Responsibilities:

  • Develop and implement sales plans to meet company targets.
  • Train and mentor sales representatives.
  • Build and maintain relationships with key clients.

Skills and Qualifications: 5+ years of experience in sales, strong leadership and communication skills.

Reporting Structure: Reports to the VP of Sales and collaborates with the Marketing team.

Content Marketing Manager

Purpose: To develop and execute our content strategy to engage customers and drive brand awareness.

Key Responsibilities:

  • Create blog posts, social media content, and email campaigns.
  • Collaborate with the design team for visuals.
  • Analyze content performance and adjust strategies accordingly.

Skills and Qualifications: 3+ years of experience in content marketing, excellent writing skills, and proficiency in SEO.

Reporting Structure: Reports to the Director of Marketing and works closely with the Sales team.
